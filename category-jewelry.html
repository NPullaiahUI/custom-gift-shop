Jewelry Care Instructions

Cleaning

Use a soft cloth and mild soap to clean your jewelry. Avoid harsh chemicals and abrasive materials.

Storage

Store jewelry in a dry place, preferably in individual pouches to prevent scratching.

Water Exposure

Remove jewelry before swimming, showering, or any water activities to prevent tarnishing.

Maintenance

Regular professional cleaning and inspection will keep your jewelry looking its best.
